Home Columbiana County Sheriff's Office (330) 424-7255 Welcome to the Columbiana County Sheriff's Office Our Vision We will be an office committed to citizen safety and satisfaction, while building the trust and confidence of the people we serve. Our Mission To improve the quality of life in Columbiana County by reducing crime, fear of crime and disorder; by working with all the citizens to preserve life, maintain rights and freedoms, protect property and to promote partnership with the community. Our Values We, the members of the Columbiana County Sheriff's Office, will provide high levels of leadership, professionalism and integrity in all we do. We will lead by seeking citizen and employee ideas for continuous improvement of our policies and services. We will show our professionalism by providing responsibly managed efficient services that will reinforce community strengths and yet still allow us to pursue criminal offenders. Finally, we will show our integrity by respecting the rights of all people and by treating them as we want to be treated. We will constantly strive to put these ideals into action.
