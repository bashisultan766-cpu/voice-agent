VISION: Continue to provide a high level of protection and preparedness for our community through training, education and professional standards for all members of the Weston County Sheriff’s Office. MESSAGE: The Weston County Sheriff’s Office purpose is to strive for the best Peace Officer protection for the community as well as providing a safe, sanitary and secure Detention facility for those inmates housed at the Weston County Sheriff’s Office. Our professional standard of operating in all facets of the Weston County Sheriff’s Office is paramount in continuing the high level of confidence that our community enjoys with the Office of the Sheriff.
