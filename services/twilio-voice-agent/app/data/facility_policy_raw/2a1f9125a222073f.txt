Our Patrol Area The Deputies of Monroe County oversee an expansive area encompassing approximately 457 square miles of diverse landscapes and communities. The Sheriff’s Office consistently addresses a wide variety of incidents, including but not limited to Domestic Violence, Assaults, Threats, Breaking and Entering, Thefts, and numerous other offenses that occur on a daily basis. In addition to handling these incidents, the office plays a vital role in providing essential protective services to the residents of Monroe County. This includes executing official Court Documents and ensuring the safety and well-being of the community through proactive policing strategies and community engagement efforts. Monroe County is strategically situated in Southeast Ohio along the picturesque banks of the Ohio River, bordered by Belmont, Noble, and Washington Counties. This unique location provides the county with rich cultural heritage and natural beauty, making it an important part of the region’s charm and an area where residents can feel a deep sense of pride and connection.
