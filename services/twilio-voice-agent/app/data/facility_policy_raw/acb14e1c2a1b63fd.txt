Davie County Sheriff’s Office consists of approximately 150 employees (sworn and non-sworn) who are dedicated to public service in the Davie County community. The Office is separated into divisions, which work together to serve the citizens of the county. These include Patrol, Criminal Investigations, Narcotics, Civil, the Davie County Detention Center and Davie County Animal Services. ​ Established in 1837, with the Sheriff as the only officer, the Davie County Sheriff’s Office has continued throughout the years to expand and improve its services to meet the needs of the growing community.
