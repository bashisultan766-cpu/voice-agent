Inmate Mail All personal mail will be digitally delivered via tablet's and no physical mail will be accepted by the facility. To ensure delivery, the sender must include the facility name and state, as well as the inmate's full name and inmate number. The sender's full name and physical address must be clearly written on the top left corner of the envelope. Example: Curry County, NM John Smith, #01234 PO BOX 247 Phoenix, MD 21131 Packages sent to the facility for inmates will not be accepted.
