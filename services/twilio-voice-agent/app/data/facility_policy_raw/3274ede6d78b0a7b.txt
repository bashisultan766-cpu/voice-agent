LEARN MORE About the Sheriff’s Office The Calhoun County Sheriff’s Office serves as the primary law enforcement agency for Calhoun County, Alabama. As a constitutional office of the State of Alabama, the Sheriff enforces state law, protects life and property, and maintains order throughout the county. In addition to patrol and investigative operations, the office manages jail administration, provides court security, executes warrant service, and handles civil process. Together, these responsibilities ensure the justice system functions efficiently and court orders are carried out properly. Professional Service and Innovation Beyond enforcement duties, the Sheriff’s Office emphasizes professionalism and innovation. Deputies complete ongoing training so they can respond effectively to emergencies, conduct thorough investigations, and address community concerns with confidence. Moreover, the agency collaborates closely with municipal police departments, emergency responders, schools, and civic organizations. Through these partnerships, law enforcement leaders share resources, improve coordination, and strengthen public safety efforts across Calhoun County. Community Partnership Public safety depends on informed and engaged citizens. Therefore, the Sheriff’s Office encourages residents to report suspicious activity, remain aware of safety information, and participate in community initiatives. At the same time, deputies work proactively to prevent crime rather than simply react to it. By combining enforcement, prevention, and community outreach, the Calhoun County Sheriff’s Office continues to build trust while maintaining a strong presence throughout Calhoun County, Alabama.
