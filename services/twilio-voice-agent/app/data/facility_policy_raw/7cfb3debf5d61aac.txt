Mason County Sheriff Department Home Tax Information Send Us Tips Open Records Welcome to the Mason County Sheriff Office's Website Sheriff Ryan R Swolsky Mission The Mason County Sheriff’s Office mission is to provide social order while protecting the liberty within the framework of the Constitution of the United States of America with reasonable direction and maximum cooperation from the public. This mission can be accomplished through crime suppression, community outreach, and constitutional awareness: Crime Suppression Maximizing our investigative capabilities through technology and data to prevent, reduce, and solve crime in the community. Continuing communication and cooperation with surrounding law enforcement agencies. Utilizing a common-sense impartial approach to policing in alignment with local, state, and federal law. Community Outreach Continuing positive partnerships with all schools in Mason County. Supporting, training, and educating local business, churches, organizations with public safety needs. Maintaining positive interactions with all people we serve. Constitutional Awareness Recruiting, selecting, and retaining constitutionally educated deputies and staff Increasing external education and awareness to the public of the role the U.S. Constitution and the safeguards inscribed therein.
