Duties of the sheriff include: Providing security for the operation of county and district courts; Enforcing traffic laws, other county ordinances and other state laws; Service of process; Accepting bail for prisoners in his custody; Conducting sales of seized and unclaimed properties; and Taking charge of and responsibility for the county jail and prisoners.
