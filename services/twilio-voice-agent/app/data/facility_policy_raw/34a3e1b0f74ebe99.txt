Website Sign In Search Home Government Departments Sheriff's Office Sheriff's Office Responsibilities The essential purpose of the Henry County Sheriff's Office is to provide service and protection to the citizens of Henry County. We will provide quality services in partnership with our community in order to enhance the well-being of the citizens of Henry County. The Henry County Sheriff's Office operates 24 hours a day, seven days a week to provide law enforcement services to the citizens of Henry County. Note Please note this website is not able to connect you directly to any emergency services. In case of emergency, dial 911 or contact your local emergency agency.
