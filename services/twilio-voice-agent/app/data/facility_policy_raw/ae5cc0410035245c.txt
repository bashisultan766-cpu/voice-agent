Website Sign In Search Home Government Sheriff Sheriff The Comanche County Sheriff's Office is dedicated to serving our community by preserving peace and enhancing quality of life through trusted service, principled leadership, and strong community collaboration. We are committed to treating every individual with dignity, respect, and fairness, recognizing that trust is earned through consistent integrity and accountability. Guided by the Constitution and the rule of law, we protect the rights of all people impartially and without prejudice. Our duty is to safeguard life and property, reduce crime, and foster a safe, supportive environment for our community. We also ensure the secure, humane care of those in our custody. Every action we take reflects our core values—serving others first, leading with empathy, and striving for excellence in a fiscally responsible manner.
