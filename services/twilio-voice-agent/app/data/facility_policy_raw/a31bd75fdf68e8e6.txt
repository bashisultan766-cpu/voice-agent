Sheriff | Belknap County, NH Skip to Main Content Search Home Departments Sheriff Sheriff In keeping with our tradition of excellence, it is the mission of the Belknap County Sheriff's Office to provide professional and skilled law enforcement and support services to the citizens and visitors of Belknap County. The members of the Office are committed to protecting and serving the community while fulfilling our role as guardians of safety and freedom. Core Values In carrying out our mission, the members of the Office will adhere to these values and guiding principles: Excellence in Service Members will continuously strive to achieve the highest levels of competency in their duties. They will conduct themselves in a manner that reflects excellence in this profession. The Office will continually strive to provide highly qualified, trained and educated personnel to serve our community. Professional Integrity All activities of the members of the Office will be based on the fundamental principle that we are held to a higher standard in our role as guardians of society’s safety and freedoms. Collaborative Approach In order to provide effective and efficient public safety, the Office will use the team approach to problem solving, both within the Office and with outside agencies. Accountability to the Public Members of the Office will serve in a professional manner that will build public confidence and trust.
