While property searching by Owner Name will be successful under most circumstances, there may be some properties that will not be found due to alternative name configurations. (i.e. the name could be recorded as “John Doe” or “Doe John”. Please try both ways.) The address can also be used as search criteria although mailing address and street address may not always be the same. View your tax bill through the Tax Bill Web Portal . Property Tax Pay Schedule 2025 - 2026 If Paid/Postmarked by November 30, 2025, 2% Discount If Paid/Postmarked by December 31, 2025, Face Amount If Paid/Postmarked by January 31, 2026, 5% Penalty If Paid/Postmarked by April 15, 2026, 21% Penalty All Property Taxes not paid/postmarked by April 15, 2026, will be turned over to the County Clerk as delinquent.
