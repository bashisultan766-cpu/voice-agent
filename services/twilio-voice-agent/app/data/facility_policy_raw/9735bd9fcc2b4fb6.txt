House medium security, general population offenders and support an extensive farming operation which provides vegetables, meats and milk for the correctional facilities. Supports Georgia State Prison by providing meals for their prison population. Provides counseling programs and educational opportunities to offenders. Sex-Offender release site. HOUSING: Consists of six buildings each containing four dormitories: 23 double occupancy cells are used for administrative segregation and 24 cells are used for disciplinary isolation. In addition, a cell is used for mental health observation. This facility also houses a C.E.R.T. Team.
