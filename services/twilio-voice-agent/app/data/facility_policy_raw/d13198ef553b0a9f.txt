POSITIVE RABIES CASE IN LAMPASAS COUNTY Please make sure that your pets are current on their Rabies Vaccinations. These cannot be purchased over the counter and given by owners, only a Veterinarian can give them. Rabies is still very much an issue in our county and surrounding counties. Making sure your pets are current will not only keep them safe, but keep your family safe from exposure as well. A raccoon tested POSITIVE in the vicinity of County Road 3365 in May, 2023. Please keep an eye out and report any animals acting strangely to Animal Control. And again DOUBLE CHECK the rabies vaccinations on your pets.
