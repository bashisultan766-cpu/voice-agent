Website Sign In Search Home Divisions Detention Center Inmate Mail Inmate Mail Persons wishing to correspond with an inmate at the Teton County Detention Center may do so by placing the inmate's full name on a stamped envelope and mailing it to the following address: Teton County Detention Center Attn Inmate: P.O. Box 1885 Jackson, WY 83001 All packages must be preapproved. Packages that are not pre-approved will be returned to the sender or discarded if no return address is provided.
