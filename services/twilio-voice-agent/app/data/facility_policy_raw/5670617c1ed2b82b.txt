Our facility offers free onsite video visits, as well as remote video visits. Before scheduling a visit, you must create an account on GettingOut.com. Once the account is approved by the facility you will be able to schedule a visit. Before visiting an inmate, please be aware of the following: * Citizens must submit a picture of a government issued identification * The Citizens’ first and last name must match the submitted identification * Citizens will have to take a headshot through the sign-up process for verification purposes * Anyone attempting to set up a video visit must be at least 18 years of age * Visitors MUST be free from incarceration a minimum of 6 months prior to any visit For questions regarding visitation you may call (423) 209-7086, or if you need help with GettingOut.com call (866) 516-0115. The visitation office is open between the hours of 10:00 am & 6:00 pm.
