Website Sign In Search Home Government SHERIFF Sheriff The Sheriff’s Office serves a population of 33,169 county residents. The Sheriff’s Office provides patrol support throughout the county covering 46 townships and cities across 1,125 square miles. Municipalities Served The cities of Little Falls, Royalton, Motley and Pierz have municipal police departments that receive primary or secondary support from the Sheriff’s Office. The Sheriff’s Office also holds criminal investigative jurisdiction at Camp Ripley, Minnesota’s largest military training base. Sheriff Larsen and his team are committed to providing the best possible services to the citizens that their office serves. Community Outreach The Sheriff’s Office continues to be actively involved with collaborative and outreach efforts throughout the county.
