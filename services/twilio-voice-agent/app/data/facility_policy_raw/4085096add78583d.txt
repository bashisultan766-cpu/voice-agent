Website Sign In Search Home Government Departments Sheriff Floyd County Sheriff's Office About the Floyd County Sheriff's Office It is the mission of the Floyd County Sheriff’s Office to protect the lives and property of the residents of this county by providing the highest quality and most professional law enforcement, correctional, and support services attainable in a cost-effective manner. The Sheriff's Office is responsible for enforcing all federal and state laws as well as county ordinances within the county. We are the primary law enforcement agency for all rural areas in Floyd County as well as the towns of Colwell, Floyd, Rudd, Rockford and Marble Rock. We also assist local law enforcement in Charles City and Nora Springs.
