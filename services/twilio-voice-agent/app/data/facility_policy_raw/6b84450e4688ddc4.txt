This mission is accomplished through people and knowledge, our most important resources. In the continuing pursuit of quality, we are guided by the following rules. Respect: We will recognize the worth, quality, diversity importance of each other, the people we serve and the Agency. Compassion: We will care about others and respect their feelings. Integrity: We will be honest and forthright and meet ethical standards. Efficiency: We will be prudent with our resources. Leadership: We will work together to set an example through leadership, which embodies respect, compassion, integrity, and efficiency.
