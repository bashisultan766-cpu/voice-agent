information, dates Sheriff’s Office The Cheboygan County Sheriff’s Office is a full service law enforcement agency that includes a road patrol, detective and recreational divisions, dive team, search and rescue, corrections, and court security. MISSION STATEMENT It is the mission of the Cheboygan County Sheriff’s Office to provide professional, quality law enforcement services to the citizens and visitors of Cheboygan County. We are dedicated to serve and protect and work in partnership with all citizens to ensure Cheboygan County is a safe place to live, work and visit.
