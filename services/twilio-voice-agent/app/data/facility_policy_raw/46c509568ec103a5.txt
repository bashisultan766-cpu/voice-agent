My staff and I are hopeful you will find our website to be informative and useful. If you can’t find something or need other assistance, please contact me or my staff. We can be contacted for emergency services through 911 and for routine assistance at the Sheriff’s Office during normal business hours. It is an honor and privilege to serve as your Sheriff. Please let me know how we may provide you and your family with the best service possible. Our men and women are proud to be a part of county government and to be serving you, our citizens.
