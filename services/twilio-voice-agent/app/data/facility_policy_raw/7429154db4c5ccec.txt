The White County Jail is responsible for the short and long term care and custody of non-sentenced and sentenced inmates and inmates sentenced to work release or other periodic imprisonment. Corrections Officers are fully trained and certified by the State of Illinois to identify potential safety, medical, or mental health risks while performing job duties with the highest professional standard. The White County Jail is located at 108 N. Main Cross St. MISSION STATEMENT The purpose of the White County Jail is to provide humane, safe, legally compliant, secure, and sanitary detention and incarceration of properly supervised pretrial and post-trial inmates while being cost effective and professional for the safety of the citizens of White County.
