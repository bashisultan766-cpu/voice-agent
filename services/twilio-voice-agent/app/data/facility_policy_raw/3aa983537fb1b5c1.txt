In addition to the Sheriff’s Office, the Emergency Communications Center serves as the countywide Public Safety Answering Point, providing dispatch services for Flagler County Fire Rescue, the Palm Coast Fire Department, the Flagler Beach Police Department, the Flagler Beach Fire Department, the Bunnell Police Department, and the Bunnell Volunteer Fire Department.
