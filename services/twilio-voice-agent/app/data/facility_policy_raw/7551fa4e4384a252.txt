Previously, he was assistant warden at Central Arizona Florence Correctional Complex. Wead joined CoreCivic in 2004 as a correctional officer at formerly CoreCivic-managed Florence Correctional Center, where he also served as senior correctional officer and assistant shift supervisor. He was promoted to shift supervisor at Eloy Detention Center. In 2015, he was promoted to assistant chief of security at Central Arizona Florence, and later to complex chief of security.
