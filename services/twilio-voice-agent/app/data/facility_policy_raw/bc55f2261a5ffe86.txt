The Courthouse, located in Clinton, was completed in 1897 and is currently in use today. The County is home to 14 communities and 46,460 residents. The County is administered by a three-member Board of Supervisors who are elected for four-year terms. Other elected officials include the Attorney , Auditor , Recorder , Sheriff , and Treasurer .
