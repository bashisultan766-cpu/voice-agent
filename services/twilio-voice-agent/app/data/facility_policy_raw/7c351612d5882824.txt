Funding Inmate Accounts There is a Kiosk in the lobby of the Sheriffs Department to deposit money for the inmates. The Kiosk accepts both cash and credit card. Wisconsin Statewide VINE System The WI Statewide VINE system is a service through which victims of crime can use the telephone or Internet to search for information regarding the custody status of their offender and to register to receive telephone and e-mail notification when the offenders custody status changes. The VINE toll-free number for the WI Statewide VINE system is 888-868-4631. This service is provided to assist crime victims and other community members who have a right to know about the offenders currently in the custody of the Sheriffs Department.
