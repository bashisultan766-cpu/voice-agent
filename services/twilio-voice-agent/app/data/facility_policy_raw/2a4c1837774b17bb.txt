Website Sign In Search Home Justice Sheriff Sheriff As a life-long resident of this great county, I am humbled to serve as your Sheriff. We work diligently to ensure your safety and well-being, while maintaining the highest standards of conduct for ourselves. All that we do is built upon the foundational pillars of accountability, professionalism, quality, work ethic, and cooperation. The Lawrence County Sheriff’s Office understands that we serve you, and we take great pride in our ability to do so. We have a dedicated staff of both sworn and civilian employees who I am proud to support. Our jail, transport, dispatch, and administrative staff may be behind the scenes, but they are vital components to public safety in Lawrence County. Furthermore, the patrol deputies and detectives are well-trained, knowledgeable public servants who strive to ensure you receive the highest level of service possible. The Lawrence County Sheriff’s Office considers it an honor and a privilege to serve in our roles. Working together as a team, with you, gives me great optimism for Lawrence County. We all want the safest communities for our families, children, loved ones, and family. Our goal, and our expectation, is to provide just that. Greg Day, Sheriff Sheriff’s App It is our privilege to offer the “Lawrence County Sheriff’s Office App”, which is available for download on both Apple and Android devices. This app will provide you with information concerning all things related to the Sheriff’s Office such as sex offenders in the county, incarcerated individuals, weather and emergency notifications, links to Sheriff’s Office job postings, inmate commissary and communication services, school safety information, anonymous tip features, and much more.
