Home - Peach County Sheriff SHERIFF ROBERT "BUCK" SHANNON The Peach County Sheriff’s Office is a respected professional law enforcement agency lead by Sheriff Robert "Buck" Shannon. Elected in 2024, Sheriff Shannon took office in January 2025. The office is staffed with 63 total employees and 31 sworn officers. Deputies patrol 151 square miles and serve nearly 27,000 residents. They also patrol I-75, one of the longest continuous six-lane freeways in the United States with two Peach County exits. “It has been an honor and privilege serving this community in the Sheriff’s office.” Jail Information The Peach County Jail is a state-of-the-art facility.
