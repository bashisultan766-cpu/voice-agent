Website Sign In Search Home Our County Departments Sheriff's Office Sheriff's Office The purpose of the Calumet County Sheriff’s Office is protection to the citizens of Calumet County, including prevention, detection, apprehension, prosecution, and detention of those criminal or civil persons who violate state or local laws. The department consists of the following divisions: Communications Center Corrections Law Enforcement Patrol Investigations Law Enforcement Records Civil Process and Foreclosures Records Sheriff Brett Bowe Email The mission of the Calumet County Sheriff's Office is to enhance the quality of life by providing proactive, efficient, and professional law enforcement services.
