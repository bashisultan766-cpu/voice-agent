OUR MISSION STATEMENT – The Bennington County Sheriff's Department exists to serve all people with respect, fairness, and sensitivity. Empowered by the community to protect the lives, property, and rights of all people and to enforce the laws impartially, we will provide quality service in partnership with the community. We will strive to attain the highest degree of ethical behavior and professional conduct at all times. OUR VISION – Through innovative thinking and goal setting, we will deliver unparalleled service to the citizens of Bennington County. This vision is built on a foundation of organizational transparency, individual accountability, collaborative partnerships, and trust. Guided by our core values: professionalism, education, integrity, and perseverance, we expect all employees to demonstrate solid devotion to these principles.
