Waco Crime Stoppers will pay up to $2,000 if your tip leads to arrest of a wanted fugitive or helps solve a case of a felony offense that leads to a felony arrest. You can submit ANONYMOUS tips one of three ways: 1. Call 753-HELP(4357) 2. Go to wacocrimestoppers.org 3. Or download the P3 TIPS app on your mobile device You can also just click on the QR Code. Inmate Visitation The Shepherd Mullens Correctional Visitation Center will be open Sundays and Mondays only. On April 5, 2026 visitation will be moved to the McLennan County Jail - Bond Office Lobby. The hours of operation have changed. Sunday it will be 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Monday it will be 12:00 PM to 8:00 PM. Registration ends at 4:30 PM on Sunday and 7:30 PM on Monday. All Inmates are allowed 2 visits of 30 minutes each visit. Visitors are required to be on the visitation list and have their account with Getting Out to visit in the facility. Remote visitation will now be available Tuesday through Saturday from 1:00 PM to 9:00 PM on the Getting Out & Go Visit Apps. IMPORTANT: Inmate Mailing Address Will Change on October 17, 2022 . Effective November 1 , 2022, all personal mail will be digitally delivered to your incarcerated friend/loved one via the GettingOut tablet.
