The King County Sheriff’s Office (KCSO) understands that these issues can create fear and uncertainty, especially for immigrant and mixed-status families. We outline how we address these concerns, clarify the responsibilities of our deputies, and explain the steps we take to ensure our work remains transparent, community centered, and separate from federal civil immigration enforcement.
