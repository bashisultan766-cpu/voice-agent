Facebook Instagram Nextdoor Linked In HCSO Halifax County Sheriff's Office You are here: Home Inmate Search Inmate Search The purpose of the Inmate Search is to promote public safety and welfare while providing community access to selected offender information, consistent with the spirit and intent of the North Carolina Public Records Act. We make every effort to keep the information updated and accurate, however, this information may change quickly. Furthermore, certain offenders, at the discretion of the Halifax County Sheriff’s Office and other law enforcement agencies, may be excluded from the web site. “Released Inmates” searches are limited to the previous 24 hour time frame.
