Carter County Sheriff's Office Oklahoma News & Press Bookings Booked: Booked: About the Carter County Sheriff's Office The Carter County Sheriff's Office is dedicated to serving the people of Carter County, Oklahoma, a region known for its rich heritage, close-knit communities, and scenic landscapes. Our county is home to hardworking individuals who value safety, integrity, and neighborly support—qualities that make Carter County a great place to live, work, and visit. Our mission is to protect and serve with professionalism, integrity, and respect. We are committed to upholding the law, ensuring public safety, and fostering strong relationships built on trust and transparency. Through proactive law enforcement, crime prevention, and community engagement, we strive to create a secure environment where every resident and visitor feels safe and supported. We work closely with local agencies, first responders, and community organizations to provide essential public safety services, respond to emergencies, and address concerns that matter most to our residents. Whether you’re a lifelong member of the community or just passing through, we encourage you to explore our resources, stay informed, and reach out whenever assistance is needed.
