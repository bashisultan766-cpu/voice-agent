We are committed to public safety by preserving the peace, and preventing crimes while protecting the constitutional rights of all those we serve. We perform our duties with compassion and integrity, while maintaining high ethical and moral conduct at all times. We will establish a reputation for responsiveness, competency, decisiveness and equality, which will inspire confidence in the Sheriff’s Department for all residents of Crawford County. A program that allows citizens to dispose of old prescription drugs. There is a box we had made that looks like a mail box and you simply bring your old drugs to the county detention center at 4235 Alma Hwy in Van Buren, AR. Open the lid and drop the drugs in. We will then dispose of the drugs. Please no liquids, trash, or syringes. Any day or time 24/7 Need to talk? Please visit our offices and ask to talk to one of our deputies. We will do our best to assist you.
