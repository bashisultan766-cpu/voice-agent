CCTC currently houses the Road to Recovery (R2R) substance abuse treatment program for male offenders only. Road to Recovery (R2R) For more information, please visit Road to Recovery . CCTC offers other treatment and programs to supplement the Road to Recovery (R2R), including: Cognitive Behavior Therapy (CBT), Bible study, parenting classes, Alcoholics Anonymous and Narcotics Anonymous.
