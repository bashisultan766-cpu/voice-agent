We believe a safe community can only exist through a partnership with our employees, residents, businesses and other public entities. Pledge We pledge to provide all services in an expeditious, effective and courteous manner while encouraging community participation in the development of goals and objectives for our organization. We will strive to be leaders and a national model in all aspects of law enforcement, and homeland security.
