Detention Division | Rockwall County-Official Website Skip to Main Content Search Home County Offices Sheriff's Office Detention Division Detention Division About The Rockwall County Detention Center is the only jail in the county. It accepts prisoners from 8 law enforcement agencies. The facility can hold 444 male and female inmates. There are 99 staff members working at the detention center. It is a tobacco-free facility. Inmates are accused or convicted of crimes like: Homicide Burglary Theft Drug trafficking Assault Inmates Inmate Worker Program Inmates do jobs like: Cooking Cleaning Maintenance Using inmate labor helps reduce costs for the facility. Inmates also help with maintenance in other parts of the county. Only low-risk inmates can work; those with violent behavior or sexual crimes cannot. Inmate Average Stay The average stay is about 70 days. Inmates can stay longer depending on their sentence, but usually no more than 1 year.
