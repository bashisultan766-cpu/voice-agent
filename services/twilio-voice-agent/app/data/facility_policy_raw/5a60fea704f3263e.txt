Website Sign In Search Home Departments Sheriff's Office Corrections Inmate Packages Inmate Packages LIST OF ITEMS INMATES MAY RECEIVE/ INCOMING PACKAGES Inmates will be allowed to receive the following items in incoming packages. ITEMS MUST BE PURCHASED FROM AND MAILED TO THE FACILITY BY A COMPANY WHOSE ORDINARY BUISNESS INCLUDES THE SALE AND SHIPPING OF SUCH ITEMS: 1 Wedding band (no stones or protrusions) 5 paperback books and/ or magazines Bible Legal books and legal materials Religious articles: (Rosary Beads, Kufi, Prayer Beads, Yarmulke, Tefillin, Religious metals on chains (chains not to exceed 18 inches in length, not to exceed $50.00 in value, have no stones or protrusions, not to exceed 2" in diameter), prayer robe, Guthra, prayer rug, Talit, Talit Katan Current newsstand publications excluding newspapers which are provided by the facility for inmate use Non-Polaroid, unaltered photographs only (no Polaroids will be allowed) Clothing items: 3 white sports bras (females only) 2 pair white long johns (shirts and bottoms) Clothing items will have the inmate's name clearly written in black permanent magic marker by the receiving Officer. This will ensure your clothing doesn't become confused with another inmate's property. Clothing for inmate's court appearance (only when court ordered) 1 pair of eye glasses or contact lenses and their case 1 pair dentures Only the items listed below may be dropped off at the facility, and will only be received Monday through Friday between the hours of 8:00 A.M. and 4:00 P.M.
