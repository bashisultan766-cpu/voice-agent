Lillard UnderSheriff George A. jackson Community focused WELCOME The Montrose County Sheriff’s Office warmly welcomes you to our website. Whether you are a resident, business, or interested law enforcement candidate, we hope you will find the information on this website helpful. The men and women of this agency are ready to assist at any time, providing high-quality law enforcement service and envisioning a safe and thriving community for everyone within Montrose County. We are community-focused, meaning we recognize it is the community of Montrose County that empowers and entrusts us to address safety and well-being concerns within our community. While arrests will always be a part of what we do, we believe crime prevention is the key to a lasting impact on crime and the quality of life experiences of our community. Our goal is the reduction in crime. Our foundation is based upon Honesty, Integrity, and Service in all that we do, with our focus being on the community that we serve.
