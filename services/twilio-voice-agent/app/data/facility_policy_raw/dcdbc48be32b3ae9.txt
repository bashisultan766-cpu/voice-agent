It is an honor and privilege to serve the residents and visitors of St. Lucie County as your Sheriff. At the St. Lucie County Sheriff’s Office, we are a purpose driven organization committed to protecting our community and providing professional service through our Standards of Performance and Core Values of doing what is right, doing the best we can, and treating people the way we would want to be treated, the Golden Rule. Our mission is larger than law enforcement. It is about building trust, strengthening partnerships, and working together to make St. Lucie County the safest county in Florida. Every member of this organization is committed to serving others with integrity, accountability, transparency, and excellence. This website is designed to provide important public safety information, agency services, community resources, and greater access to your Sheriff’s Office. We believe strong communication and community engagement are essential to maintaining a safe and connected community. On behalf of the men and women of the St. Lucie County Sheriff’s Office, thank you for your continued trust, support, and partnership. We are proud to serve this community and remain committed to setting a standard for others to follow. Sheriff Richard R.
