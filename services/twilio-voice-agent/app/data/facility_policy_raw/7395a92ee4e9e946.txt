Website Sign In Search Home Government Departments Departments J-Z Sheriff's Office Sheriff's Office Mission Statement The Churchill County Sheriff's Office's mission is to provide effective and efficient law enforcement and public safety services to the residents and visitors of Churchill County. We strive to accomplish this mission in a fair and consistent manner with a professional and highly trained staff. In Memoriam The Sheriff's Office remembers Sheriff Richard Hickox who passed away in April 2026. He served this department for 28 years, rising through the ranks. He was elected in 2018 and served as sheriff for seven years before his untimely passing. We remember his passion for this department and dedication to this community. Rest in peace, Richard, we have the watch. Mobile App A free mobile app from the Churchill County Sheriff's Office is now available. Keep up-to-date on a variety of local law enforcement issues including jail information, inmate searches, submitting tips to law enforcement, road information via NDOT, weather, domestic violence services and elder protective services.
