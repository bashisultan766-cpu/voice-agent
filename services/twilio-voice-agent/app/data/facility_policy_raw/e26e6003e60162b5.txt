About the Sheriff Travis L. Tucker: Was elected to Sheriff in 2019 as the 15 th Duchesne County Sheriff. Has been in law enforcement for over twenty-six years. He graduated from the Utah Peace Officers Standards and training in 1994 and was hired by the Duchesne County Sheriff’s Office in 1995. During his career he has held many positions ranging from correction deputy, detective, Lieutenant and the Undersheriff. He holds the position as Director for the UBtech Police Academy in Roosevelt educating new recruits for a career in law enforcement. Prevention, Community and Cooperation has been his leading guidelines through out his law enforcement career. Our Mission To protect the lives, property, and rights of all people, to maintain order, and to enforce the law. This will be achieved through the efforts of our experienced and well trained officers and staff of the Duchesne County Sheriff’s Office. Together, we will improve and maintain the quality of life we enjoy to ensure our County is a safe place to live, work, and visit. Our Vision To set goals and objectives to meet the high standards of law enforcement services in accordance with our mission. To exemplify our core values in accordance with our Code of Ethics. To operate in a fiscally responsible manner. To uphold the Constitution of the United States of America by enforcing the rights guaranteed to the citizens of the County through lawful deterrents to crime.
