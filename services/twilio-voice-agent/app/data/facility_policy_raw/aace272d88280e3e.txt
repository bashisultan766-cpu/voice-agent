Box 6 New Bloomfield, PA 17068 Email: sheriffsoffice@perryco.org Phone: (717) 582-5123 Fax: (717) 582-5115 Hours: Monday – Friday 8am to 4pm Mission Statement It is the mission of the Perry County Sheriff’s Office to protect and serve the citizens and visitors of Perry County with fairness, compassion, and respect. We pledge to work hard daily to achieve excellence in our profession while incorporating the core values of integrity, professionalism, and honor in every phase of our responsibilities. We are committed to maintaining a law enforcement presence in our community, providing security to the courts, assisting other county agencies in times of need/risk, protecting county-owned/leased buildings and property, executing orders of court, and issuing various licenses. Above all, we will remember our sworn oath to protect the constitutional rights of all the people while working together to keep persons free from crime and never forgetting the right to live in a peaceful environment. Responsibilities Providing security to the courts and other county properties Providing a law enforcement presence to county offices in times of need/risk Provide law enforcement assistance and support to other law enforcement agencies Provide safe and secure transportation of prisoners for court appearances Service of warrants issued by the courts Serve and enforcement of court orders, writs of summons, complaints, money judgements and injunctions Conducting Real Estate and Personal Property Sales Issuing Licenses to Carry Concealed Firearms, Licenses to Sell Firearms and Licenses to Sell Precious Metals Enforce violations of the PA Crimes and Vehicle Code Provide education, assistance and awareness to the public through community service programs Provide Finger printing services (for a $10.00 fee) How to respond when an active shooter is in your vicinity If you would like more information after reading this, you may contact the Sheriff Dept. at (717) 582-5123.
