How Do I Use Ameelio? ⤢ Note: If you do not have access to use the Ameelio app, you may contact the prison directly to discuss manually scheduling an in-person visit. Please call (712) 695-7140 and speak to the incarcerated individual's counselor. Note: Per department visitation policy, all visitors upon entrance to the institutions shall be subject to search. Security and contraband control are critical operational requirements, necessitating a search of all visitors by trained correctional staff. Searches may include, but are not limited to, a pat down, metal detectors, electronic ion scanners, other electronic devices, or visual searches. If visitors refuse to comply with the department visitation policy, entrance to the visiting room will be denied.
