We will do so through an open partnership with our citizens, open communications and accountability will make this mission successful. Vision Statement The vision of the Cass County Sheriff's Office is to be a leader in law enforcement. A cooperative spirit in which police officers and citizens work together to solve common problems will be the hallmark of our office. Values Community Integrity Service Commitment Professionalism Sheriff deputies are on duty 24 hours a day . If you have information about a crime we are investigating or have information about the location of a wanted person, please call our Investigation Section.
