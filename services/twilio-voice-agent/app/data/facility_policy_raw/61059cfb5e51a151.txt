Sheriff's Office | deuelcountysd top of page Sheriff's Office The Deuel County Sheriff's Office covers 637 square miles and serves over 4,312 people with a sheriff, five deputies, and one administrative assistant. The office duties include 24/7 electronic alcohol monitoring, answering calls of service, background fingerprinting, civil/criminal paper service, issues concealed pistol permits, investigates accidents and crimes, transports prisoners, community safety talks, security of the courts, 911 coordinator, traffic control during events, traffic enforcement and works within the communities and schools throughout Deuel County.
