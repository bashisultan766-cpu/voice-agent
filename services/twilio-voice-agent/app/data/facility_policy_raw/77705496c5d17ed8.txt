Inmate Mail | Carroll County, NH Skip to Main Content Search Home Departments Department of Corrections Inmates Inmate Mail Inmate Mail As of November 1, 2024, the Carroll County House of Corrections does not accept inmate personal non-privileged mail. All incoming non-privileged mail will be returned to the sender or, in the absence of a complete return address, disposed of. Inmates will not be notified of returned or disposed of mail.
