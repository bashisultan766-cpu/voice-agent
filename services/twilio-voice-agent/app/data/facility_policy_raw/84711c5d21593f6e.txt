Website Sign In Search Home Your Government Sheriff's Office Sheriff's Office Citizens of Cowley County, The Cowley County Sheriff's Office is proud to serve the citizens of Cowley County. The Sheriff is responsible for all service of civil process from the District Court, court security and the Cowley County Jail. The Deputies respond to calls for service and conduct traffic activities as well as respond to traffic accidents. Mission Statement In order to provide professional law enforcement services to the citizens of Cowley County, the Cowley County Sheriff's Office will engage in positive, transparent communication with the public. A positive work environment for employees of the Sheriff's Office will be gained through mutual trust and open dialogue where the employee's ideas will be taken seriously and their dedication appreciated. Professional Law Enforcement Services The members of the Cowley County Sheriff's Office is made up of professionals who uphold high standards of ethics and integrity. Positive & Transparent Communication with the Public We will work with the communities that we serve in an effort to have positive relationships and open communication. Positive Work Environment Gained Through Mutual Trust & Dialogue The Sheriff will communicate a clear leadership direction to the members of the Sheriff's Office and provide adequate training for advancement.
