Shepard, Asst. Jail Administrator Ext. 265 Wayne Cross, Administrative Assistant Ext.223 Hancock County Sheriff’s Office is one of sixteen in Maine. The county population is around 55,000 in the off-season but triples during the tourist season. Hancock County Sheriff’s Office works with the Maine State Police in an agreement for coverage. Hancock is divided into three geographic zones. The Sheriff’s Office always covers two zones, and the State Police cover one. Zone coverage is swapped weekly. The Patrol Division consists of the Sheriff, Chief Deputy, Administrative Assistant, two Lieutenants, two Detectives, four patrol Sergeants, one School Resource Officer, two airport deputy positions, the Town of Swans Island contract deputy, and patrol deputies. Specialties within the Patrol Division are: K-9 Reconstructionist UAV (Drone) Crash Mapping Maine Drug Enforcement Assignment The Civil Division consists of one full-time civil deputy and an office manager. The civil deputy is responsible for serving all civil paperwork within the county. The Corrections Division consists of one Jail Administrator, one Assistant Jail Administrator, five Sergeants, four Corporals, nine corrections officers, and five part-time officers. The facility is licensed by the State of Maine to house up to 66 inmates.
