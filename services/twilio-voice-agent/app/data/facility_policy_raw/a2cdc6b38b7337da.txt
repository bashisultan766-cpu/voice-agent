Search Home Departments Public Safety Sheriff's Office Divisions Jail Division Visitation and Communication Inmate Mail Inmate Mail the Paulding COunty jail will only accept 3" x 5" postcards as mail for currently incarcerated inmates. All inmate mail must have a return address on the Postcard and will be inspected for contraband. Inmates can only receive the following things through the mail: 3" x 5" Postcard (which can depict images) These pictures cannot contain: Nudity Violence Depictions of illegal activity An Inmate is allowed to have 3 postcard pictures in their possession at any time. If an inmate receives mail that contains anything other than the items listed above, the postcard or letter and everything in it will be returned to the sender. Any letters sent to the Paulding County Jail will remain unopened and returned to the sender.
