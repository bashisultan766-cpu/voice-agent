From the Sheriff | Wabaunsee County Sheriff The current staff of the sheriff’s office represent over 150 years of combined law enforcement experience. These are dedicated and hardworking individuals. It is the goal of this office to provide all of the citizens of Wabaunsee County quality law enforcement through community based policing. We will strive to maintain a professional sheriff’s office with dedicated and trained professional employees. The Wabaunsee County Sheriff’s Office will be providing information to all of you in an attempt to keep you informed of what is occurring in the county. We provide this information through this website and our FACEBOOK page. We encourage all citizens to be aware, informed, and vigilant. Do not hesitate to contact this office at any time with your cares and concerns. Blessings to all of you and your families and thank you for your support in our efforts.
