Our county is 837 square miles in size. The population was reported at 21,159 by the census bureau in 2010. The Vernon County Sheriff's Office is responsible for patrolling, responding to incidents and investigating crimes throughout the county along with running the county jail, serving the courts paper services, and providing bailiff services for the courts.
