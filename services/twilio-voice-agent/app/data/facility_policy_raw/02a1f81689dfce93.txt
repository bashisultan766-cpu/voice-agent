You must supply your own fingerprint cards. Call 715-373-6117 to make an appointment. Inmate Mail Inmate mail should be addressed as follows: Inmate's Name c/o Bayfield County Jail P.O. Box 115 Washburn, WI 54891 The sender's name and return address must also be printed on the envelope. Visitation Visitations are done on a first come/first serve basis by coming to the Sheriff's Office Lobby and setting up a visit in person. Please make sure to bring a picture ID with you when you arrive for your visit.
