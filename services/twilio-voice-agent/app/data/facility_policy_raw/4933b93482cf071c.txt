Audrain County Sheriff Serving Audrain County Missouri Serving Since 1836 Welcome to the Audrain County Sheriff's Office Website We are providing this information not only as a service to our citizens, but as a source of information to prospective citizens of our fine county. We hope that you will find our site to be most informative. I am proud of the services that we provide, our job performance, and especially the rate of crimes that we clear annually. We clear nearly 35% more crime than the national average. Our success is measured by the quality of our citizens and the information that they share with my deputies. We want to keep Audrain County a safe and enjoyable place to live, work, and/or go to school and we encourage you to call us with any questions or problems that you may have.
