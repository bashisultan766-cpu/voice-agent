She was residing outside of Filley, Nebraska in Gage County. She was reported to be a missing adult shortly after her last sighting in Beatrice, Nebraska. There has been no contact with her and no sightings of her anywhere in the United States since April 15, 2008. At the time, she had four juvenile children, who are now grown. One of Ann’s three jobs was as a cleaner at Southeast Community College in Beatrice, Nebraska. In addition to her job at the community college, Ann also worked at the One-Stop Country Store and for a relative’s upholstery business at the time of her disappearance. Investigators stated it’s out of character for her to leave without warning and they believe she was the victim of a homicide. Her case remains unsolved.
