Morgan County Sheriffs Office The Morgan County Sheriff's Office is located in historic Berkeley Springs, WV. We are a law enforcement agency serving and protecting our citizens by maintaining an around-the-clock law enforcement presence in the beautifully scenic Morgan County, West Virginia. Utilizing ground-breaking techniques and technologies, our divisions include patrol, prevention, criminal investigations, civil process, court, and actively playing a role in community events and government activities. We demand, and fully expect, only the best from our personnel. We pro-actively maintain an aggressive approach to serving our community, training and educational programs that further prepare the members of our office to meet this demand.
