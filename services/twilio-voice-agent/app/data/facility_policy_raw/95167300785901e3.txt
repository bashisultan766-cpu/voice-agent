Search Home Departments Sheriff's Office Sheriff's Office The Chambers County Sheriff's Office ( CCSO ) is committed to ensuring the safety and well-being of the residents of Chambers County. Our duties encompass a wide range of responsibilities aimed at maintaining public safety, upholding the law, and fostering positive community relationships. We take pride in serving you with integrity, professionalism, and a commitment to the values of Chambers County. Our mission is to create a safe and secure environment for all residents, and we welcome your partnership in achieving this goal. Duties of the Sheriff's Office Law Enforcement: We enforce state laws within Chambers County, responding to emergencies, investigating crimes, and patrolling to uphold public order. County Jail Operations: Operating the Chambers County Jail, we prioritize the secure custody of inmates while ensuring their safety and well-being. Search and Rescue: In emergency situations, particularly in rural areas, we actively participat e in search and rescue operations, including those involving missing persons or natural disasters. Traffic Enforcement: Enhancing road safety, we enforce traffic laws and contribute to the overall well-being of motorists within Chambers County. Community Policing: We are dedicated to building strong connections with our community through active engagement and collaborative efforts, fostering a sense of safety and partnership. Concealed Handgun Licensing: Processing applications for concealed handgun licenses in accordance with state laws, ensuring responsible firearm ownership within Chambers County. Warrant Services: CCSO executes arrest and search warrants, assists in targeted searches for evidence, facilitates the transportation of detainees, and maintains transparent warrant records with a balance of enforcement and respect.
