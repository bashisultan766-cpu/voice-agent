LEARN MORE James started his law enforcement career in 1992. He has spent his entire career in McClain County, serving the citizens of the county. James has held various positions within his career, but his focus has always been to help people and keep them safe. James holds an Advanced CLEET Certification and also is a Nationally Registered EMR. LEARN MORE Captain Bryan Murrell started his law enforcement career in 2001. He began his career at the Oklahoma County Sheriff’s Office Detention Center before completing his Associates Degree and becoming CLEET certified. Captain Murrell worked as a patrol officer with the Rush Springs Police Department before becoming a member of the Grady County Sheriff’s Office where he served in various roles. Captain Murrell took a brief absence from law enforcement to manage an Oil & Gas service company. Captain Murrell came back to his calling in law enforcement and served as the Chief of Police for the Dibble Police Department before being hired at the McClain County Sheriff’s Office under Sheriff Don Hewett. Captain Murrell has served as a Patrol Deputy, Detective Sergeant, and now as a Captain over operations under Sheriff Landy Offolter. Captain Murrell graduated from Newcastle High School and currently lives in Newcastle with his wife Heather of 20 years. Bryan and Heather have raised 3 daughters in Newcastle with all but the youngest graduating from Newcastle High School. Captain Murrell supervises the Operations of the McClain County Sheriff’s Office including Patrol and Investigations. He serves on the Oklahoma Internet Crimes against Children Task Force and Western District of Oklahoma Safe Trails Task Force. Captain Murrell enjoys leading the men and women of the McClain County Sheriff’s Office as well as serving the Citizens of McClain County.
