Website Sign In Search Home Departments Public Safety Sheriff Divisions Detentions Detentions The Lake County Sheriff's Office houses inmates lawfully committed while the individuals await advisement of the court or until they are able to post the bail amount, which ever is sooner. If inmates require longer than a 48-hour duration within detention facilities, Lake County Sheriff's Deputies ensure the safe transport to another facility for the duration of their stay.
