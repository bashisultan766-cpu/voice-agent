We perform our law enforcement and custody duties with honor and integrity in order to guard the public trust. Our highly trained and competent workforce demonstrate the highest standards of professionalism. We will ensure fair and equal administration of the law, safeguard civil liberties and preserve public safety with professionalism and unity of purpose while being good stewards of the public's trust.
