Mario Eldridge Facility Operations Inmate Programming Offered: GED / Library Alcohol & Drug Rehabilitation Religious Services Mental Health Counseling Medical Services The jail’s medical program (mandated by State Law) includes care by a doctor, nurses, dentist, mental health counselor, and psychiatrist. Food Services The jail kitchen staff prepare all inmate meals in-house. The cooks prepare over 1,200 meals per day. All meals are prepared based on a menu approved by the State Dietician.
