Through a partnership with the North Dakota Sheriff’s and Deputies Association, and funded by the North Dakota Department of Human Services, the Stark County Sheriff’s Office is now participating in tip411 service to allow citizens to submit anonymous tips. ​ When you submit a tip through any of the options listed below, it is sent to a third party tip service, which acts as a privacy buffer between you and our agency in order to protect your identity and your contact information. ​ This service is being provided to the public for non-emergency use. Tips may not be responded to immediately. It is not meant to be used as a substitute for calling 911 in emergency situations.
