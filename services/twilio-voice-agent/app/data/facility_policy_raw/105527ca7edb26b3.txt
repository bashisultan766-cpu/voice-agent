HANG UP IMMEDIATELY!! 1) We are now accepting License to Carry applications and renewals online at the following link: https://butlerpa.permitium.com/ccw/start This is an option that I would like to provide for your convenience. We have partnered with a 3rd party vendor (Permitium) to provide this opportunity. There is an administrative fee and a credit card fee paid to the vendor with this feature. You can always come into the office to apply or renew your License. If you choose to apply online, you will be notified about the status of the process and will be notified when to come in for your signature and picture. 2) We have a public WARRANT portal to view active warrants at https://civil.co.butler.pa.us/sheriff.warrantportal 3) We also have a public portal for Mortgage Foreclosure sale listing at https://civil.co.butler.pa.us/sheriff.salelisting/ 4) All Mortgage Foreclosure sales will be available online only at https://butler.pa.realforeclose.com/ All prospective buyers will have to register and provide a deposit to be permitted to bid. Buyers will be provided bidder training with several webinar opportunities which is sponsored by Real Auction. The buyer must allocate their funds to the Butler County sale.
