Website Sign In Search Commendations & Complaints Submit a Tip Frequently Asked Questions Community Policing Welcome To Aiken County Sheriff's Office The Sheriff's Office Mission We, the men and women of the Aiken County Sheriff’s Office, believe that our fundamental duty is to serve and protect the citizens and visitors of Aiken County with fairness, compassion, and respect. We recognize the importance of professionalism, integrity, and timeliness so that those we serve may feel secure. We provide equal enforcement and protection of the law without prejudice or favor. Our commitment is fulfilled through communication, openness, and cooperation with all members of our community. The Detention Center Mission The mission of the Aiken County Sheriff’s Office Detention Division is to enhance public safety by safely and securely managing persons entrusted into our custody. We will accomplish this by providing quality customer service, using sound ethical correctional principles, embracing diversity and building partnerships with our stakeholders.
