READ MORE Our Mission, Vision and Guiding Principles Our mission is to serve the people of Pacific County with honor and commitment to upholding the Constitution with unwavering courage. We are dedicated to serving our community with integrity, transparency, and accountability. Every action we take is guided by our steadfast commitment to doing what is right every time, even if standing alone. We are directly accountable to the People of Pacific County, and it is our privilege to serve and protect our fellow citizens.
