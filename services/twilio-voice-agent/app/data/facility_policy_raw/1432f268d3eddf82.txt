My team and I work every day to enforce the law fairly, respond quickly to emergencies, and build trust through community partnerships. Whether you need assistance, want to learn about safety programs, or simply have a question, our office is here to serve you. Our Mission We Stand For: Protection – Safeguarding the lives and property of all residents and visitors Service – Providing professional, respectful assistance to everyone in our county Justice – Enforcing laws fairly and without bias Partnership – Building relationships with the community to prevent crime before it happens Services & Resources Criminal Investigations – Solving cases and pursuing justice Patrol & Traffic Enforcement – Keeping our roads and neighborhoods safe Court Security – Ensuring safety within our judicial system Civil Process – Serving court documents efficiently and lawfully Emergency Response – Acting quickly during crises and natural disasters Community Programs – Safety education, outreach events, and prevention efforts Meet Your Sheriff Sheriff Dale Richardson is a lifelong resident of Knott County and a dedicated public servant. With years of experience in law enforcement, he leads with integrity, fairness, and a deep respect for the people he serves.
