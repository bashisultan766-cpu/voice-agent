The changes are being made to streamline the operations of the Sheriff’s Office, improve communications, and ensure the safety and well-being of inmates and staff. MailGuard is a quick and efficient way for inmates to receive letters and photos electronically. Effective 04/21/2026 , inmate postal mail must be sent to the following address: Smart Communications / Muskegon County Jail Adam Smith #123456 (example inmate ) PO Box 9135 Seminole, FL 33775-9135 Any mail received at the facility after 04/28/2026 will be returned to the sender .
