Sheriff’s Office - Oceana County Michigan Sheriff’s Office In This Section Welcome to the Oceana County Sheriff’s Office The Oceana County Sheriff’s Office, led by Sheriff Craig Mast, is staffed by deputies and corrections officers 24 hours per day, seven days a week to serve the community. Comprised of a Patrol Division as well as a Detective Bureau , the Sheriff’s Office is also responsible for overseeing the 66-bed County Correctional Facility .
