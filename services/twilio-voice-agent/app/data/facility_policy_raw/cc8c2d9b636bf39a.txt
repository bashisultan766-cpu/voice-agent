NORCOR - Northern Oregon Regional Corrections 0 Open Menu Close Menu Open Menu Close Menu Northern Oregon Regional Corrections About NORCOR Northern Oregon Regional Correctional Facilities (NORCOR) is a Regional Adult Corrections and Juvenile Detention complex that serves four counties. Setting a national precedent, the counties of Wasco, Hood River, Sherman and Gilliam merged to develop this facility. NORCOR is committed to serving these four counties and their communities.
