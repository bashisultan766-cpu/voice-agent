3. QUALIFICATIONS OF BIDDERS 
  
3.1 Before submitting a bid, the successful Vendor must be legally qualified in all respects to do 
business in the State of Arkansas.  
 
3.2 Vendor will be responsible for obtaining any and all permits required at no additional charge to 
the County. The rates bid shall include all materials and labor as required. 
 
3.3 Vendor shall furnish a minimum of three references from business of similar size and type.  
References must include name of business, name of contact, address and phone number, along  
with the type of and quantity of services performed for that business. 
 
 
 
4. REQUIREMENTS 
 
4.1 Mail must have a Fayetteville, Arkansas postmark. 
 
4.2 Mail must be picked up twice a day with the last pickup no earlier than 4:00 p.m., Monday 
through Friday. 
 
4.3 All mail must be metered, sorted and mailed on the same day collected. 
 
4.4 Monthly statement from vendor must detail all charges as detail ed in the pricing on the pricing 
proposal and provide mail processing count for each day by department.  
 
4.5 Contractor must have postal equipment and sufficient  personnel  to operate the equipment and 
complete all pickup and deliveries.  Washingt on County will be the sole judge and may require 
an on-site visit of contractor’s business at any time, before and or after award is made. 
 
4.6 Contractor will insure that the County’s mail will be processed and grouped to receive the  
  lowest possible postage rate. 
 
4.7 Contactor will have FASTforeward software and hardware to aid in processing all outgoing mail.  
Contractor must be able to scan and read all mail and apply proper bar-coding to allow the 
County to receive the lowest possible first class presort rate or Carrier Route available by law.
