Any person with information relevant to this compliance audit may confidentially* correspond with the auditor via the following address: Amanda van Arcken P.O. Box 513 Sherwood, Oregon 97140-0513 *CONFIDENTIALITY – All correspondence and disclosures during interviews with the designated auditor are confidential and will not be disclosed unless required by law. There are exceptions when confidentiality must be legally broken. Exceptions include, but are not limited to the following: if the person is an immediate danger to her/himself or others (e.g. suicide or homicide); allegations of suspected of child abuse, neglect or maltreatment; in legal proceedings where information has been subpoenaed by a court of appropriate jurisdiction. Inmate mail Procedures All personal mail will be opened and inspected. All personal mail will be scanned and distributed through our Digital Mailing Center (DMC) to the inmate’s tablet. All original mailings will be discarded once it has been scanned via DMC and set to the inmate. The following are requirements for all inmate mail received by the DMC: Mail must be addressed to the inmate using the inmate’s full name Mail must include the full name and address of the sender Mail must be 10 pages or less per envelope Page size no larger than 8.5” x 11” Photo size no larger than 5” x 7”, no polaroid photos, no photos of nude persons No items containing, depicting, or relating to sexually explicit activity, gang activity, illegal activity, violence, drug, or alcohol use, etc. No magazines, books, or packages No cash allowed No 3 rd party mail No blank envelopes, blank paper, stamps, photocopies or laminated items, stickers, internet materials, newspaper clippings, or sticky notes No illegal substances or any other items considered as contraband No originals of important documents such as birth certificates, driver’s licenses, social security cards, green cards, etc., as these items cannot be returned No original photos or other irreplaceable or valuable items Acceptable examples of how to address mail to inmate John Doe Cascade County Detention Facility John Doe 3800 Ulm North Frontage Road Great Falls, MT. 59404 Or Cascade County Sheriff’s Office John Doe 3800 Ulm North Frontage Road Great Falls, MT. 59404 Add Money to an Inmate's Account Mail in a Money Order addressed to the inmate.
