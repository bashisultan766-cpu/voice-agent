McCracken County Jail - Inmate Services We NOW offer two ways to visit – online and at the jail. To create a visiting account, go to www.inmatesales.com . (For HELP, call 877-998-5678) After an account is made, you may schedule a visit 24 or more hours out. ONSITE visits are FREE at the jail. ONLINE visits are $0.40/minute. All visits are a maximum of 20 minutes each. Inmates may receive 20 minute ONLINE visits and 2 - 20 minute ONSITE visits per week. Rules apply to both onsite and online visits. VISITS MUST BE SCHEDULED BY PHONE @ 866-340-7879 Visitation is Monday - Friday 9:00 AM - 4:30 PM, Saturday - Sunday 12:00 PM - 4:00 PM Visits are conducted through video only. We offer 2 (20) minute visits per week on site. Visitor Rules Visits are conducted through video. Conversations may be recorded. We will offer two (2) twenty (20) minute visits per week on site. Only TWO (2) adults are allowed to visit at a time. No cell phones or cameras allowed. Visitors MUST show ID and sign in for visits. Anyone under the age of 18 must be accompanied by an adult. No food or drink may be brought in by visitors. No contact orders shall be enforced during visitation. No property may be left during visits on Saturday. Any visitor appearing to be under the influence of alcohol or drugs, or any visitor who is loud or unruly will be asked to leave and may be arrested. No weapons concealed or otherwise. Inmates in isolation are only allowed visitation once a week. ANY inappropriate behaviour will NOT be tolerated. Visitations are only for the visitors (and approved, logged in guests) and inmates scheduled. Any attempt to visit outside those parameters will result in visitation termination and ban. NOTE: These rules are subject to change at the McCracken County Jail's discretion. Permitted Items Commissary is held once a week on Wednesday. Money must be on the account by 12 on Sunday. If an inmate owes money to the jail 25% of money deposited on the account will be applied to fees owed. Money put on an account for commissary to be directed to a different inmate may be confiscated by the Jail. If you wish to purchase a care pack for an inmate please go to CareACell to purchase. If you wish to leave money for an inmate you must deposit the money in the Kiosk in the front lobby or CareACell or mail in a postal money only. (no Western Union or money graham) Housing Fees Bond Fee - $5 Booking Fees - $30 County Inmate fee - $20 Medical Fees County Inmates - 100% of actual costs to the Jail for any type of medical/mental health/ dental related services or prescriptions. State Inmates - 75% of actual costs to the Jail for any type of medical/mental health/ dental related services or prescriptions. Nurse Visits - Charges will be determined by medical staff or Jailer on a case by case basis. Upon release any money on an inmate account will be applied to fees owed. Inmates released from custody owing money may sign a billing agreement and pay accordingly or be subject to civil or contempt action. Inmates participating in work release through District Court must have it approved by Court prior to being released for work. Ample travel time each way, to and from work, will be allowed. If you do not return from work on time your work release may be terminated. If alcohol/drug use is detected work release may be terminated and you may be found in Contempt of Court. Inmates who are on work release and are not at work may have their work release terminated. Persons sentenced in District Court will be charged $20 for each day spent in jail. Inmates with work release must pay fees at the end of each week before being allowed release the next week. Inmates are responsible for all medical, dental and prescription expenses. Inmates with an unpaid balance upon release must make payment arrangements prior to release. If Jail fees are not paid as arranged you may be found in Contempt of Court. Meals are served at approximately 6:30am, 12:00pm and 6:30pm. People who arrive after this time will have to wait until the next meal is served. No magazines shall be mailed to or subscribed to for delivery to the jail. How to Address Mail: Inmate Mail First and Last Name, ID# McCracken County Jail P.O.
