Jon began his law enforcement career with the Lewistown Police Department in November of 2009, before being hired by the Fulton County Sheriff’s Office in April of 2011. Jon was promoted to Sergeant of Investigations in June of 2020. Jon was elected Sheriff in November of 2022.
