Holiday Closures Our Team Matt Maschmann Sheriff Careers Civil Division Deputies Dispatch & Jailers Reserve Deputies Sex Offender Range Maps Sheriff Sales Weapon Permits FAQ Mission Statement Our mission is to provide the highest quality, full-service law enforcement to all citizens and visitors of Poweshiek County. We will achieve this goal by emphasizing training for our sworn and civilian staff. We value communication and input from the community we serve. We understand it is vital to work together with the community, in order to identify and address local issues. We will always strive to become more efficient and effective in the services we provide. We will fairly, impartially and respectfully enforce the Laws of the State of Iowa and relentlessly pursue the truth in all investigations. We value accountability from everyone inside and outside our agency. We will treat everyone with whom we interact with respect. We understand that the vast majority of citizens are good honest people who want to live their lives in a safe community. For the minority who chose to commit crimes against the people and property of Poweshiek County, we will focus our time and attention to hold you accountable in a fair, impartial and respectful manner.
