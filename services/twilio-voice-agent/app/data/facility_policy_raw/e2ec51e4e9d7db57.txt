With a dedicated team of highly skilled professionals, we strive to maintain safety, security, and order in our community. In the Community The Monterey County Sheriff's Office is deeply committed to being an active and engaged member of the community. Through various fundraisers, outreach efforts, and programs, the Sheriff’s Office strives to build positive relationships with the residents they serve. The dedication of the Sheriff’s Office to community involvement sets a shining example and ensures a collaborative effort towards a safer and more vibrant Monterey County. Occupied Vehicle in Parkfield May 22, 2026 May 22, 2026 Press Releases Team Spotlight The Drone Unit Drones have become an invaluable asset in law enforcement, as they provide an aerial perspective that was once only accessible through expensive helicopters or manned aircraft. Equipped with high-resolution cameras and thermal imaging capabilities, these unmanned aerial vehicles can swiftly cover large areas, monitor suspicious activities, and assist in search and rescue missions. By deploying drones, our department has significantly enhanced community safety, enabling us to respond swiftly to emergencies, monitor high-crime areas, and gather crucial real-time data for effective decision-making. Additionally, the use of drones not only minimizes potential risks for our personnel, but also offers cost-effective solutions that benefit both taxpayers and law enforcement agencies alike.
