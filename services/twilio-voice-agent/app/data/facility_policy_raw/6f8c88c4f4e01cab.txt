Jasper SO MESSAGE FROM THE SHERIFF: The Jasper County Sheriff’s Department consists of 46 full and part time employees who are trained and experienced in law enforcement, corrections and dispatch communications. We strive as a department to provide professional law enforcement to every citizen of Jasper County. Our department takes every opportunity to be active with the county youth and in the county school systems. Our goal is to see Jasper County grow with new industry and employment opportunities. We as a department are working for the citizens of this county and pride ourselves on being available to them. Jasper County Sheriff’s Department has a strong working relationship with our neighboring Sheriff’s Departments and State agencies and we will use all available resources to help keep our county safe. Together we are making a positive change in Jasper County.
