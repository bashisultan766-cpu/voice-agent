CONTACT REPORT A CRIME REPORT A CRIME QUICK LINKS Sheriff Doug Chapman The Sheriff’s principal duties involve the charge and custody of the County Jail. His department handles both criminal and non-criminal complaints, enforces traffic and water safety laws, as well as serves writs and other court papers. The Sheriff’s Department also transports criminals to either court or prison and handles extradition proceedings. The Sheriff is elected to a 4 year term by the voters of Shiawassee County on a partisan ballot in the November general election.
