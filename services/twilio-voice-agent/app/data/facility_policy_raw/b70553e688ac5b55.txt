Law enforcement will not contact you about: Payment related to outstanding warrants. Unpaid back taxes to the IRS. Personal financial information. Payment methods like gift cards or prepaid cards. Your social security number. Law enforcement calls are typically related to: Ongoing cases or incidents you are already aware of. Gathering information about a specific matter by providing a certain level of context. You can check the legitimacy of the call. If in doubt about a call's legitimacy, hang up and contact the 911 communication center at 651-439-9381 to confirm if an officer or deputy is genuinely trying to reach you.
