Website Sign In Search Home Government Justice Sheriff's Office Sheriff's Office Our Mission Statement The Blackford County Sheriff's Office is committed to working together with the citizens of our community to protect our future, promote justice and help improve the quality of life. We will show the highest standard of integrity and professionalism. This will be accomplished through leading by example and positive influence, which will create trust, respect, and sense of community. The members of the Blackford County Sheriff's Office will demonstrate fairness, compassion and courtesy while performing their duties. We will always provide loyal and dedicated service to our community. The Blackford County Sheriff's Office will provide devoted, proactive, and community-oriented service to all citizens of Blackford County.
