Mission Statement The mission of the Augusta-Richmond County Correctional Institution is to protect the public, victims of crime and staff and also supplement the City of Augusta-Richmond County workforce with cost-effective Inmates’ Labor. Our Vision Through the utilization of work details, rehabilitative programs, and a structured environment with a team of committed professionals, we hope to release a more responsible, educated, disciplined, and productive individual to society.
