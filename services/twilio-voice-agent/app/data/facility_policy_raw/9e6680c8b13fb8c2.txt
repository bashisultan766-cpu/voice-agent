Legal Correspondence To send legal correspondence to an inmate, address to: Butler County Prison C/O Inmate’s Full Name 202 S Washington St. Butler, PA 16001 Publications To send Paperback Books (No used books), Magazines and Newspapers they must be mailed to the inmates and come directly from the publisher.
