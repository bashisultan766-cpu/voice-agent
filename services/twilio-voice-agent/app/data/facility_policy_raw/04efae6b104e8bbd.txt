To send mail to an inmate, please include the inmate’s name, DCDC Number and the address of the DC Jail (1901 D Street, SE, Washington, DC 20003). All incoming mail will be opened and searched for contraband. Note: there is no limit to the amount of mail an inmate can send or receive.
