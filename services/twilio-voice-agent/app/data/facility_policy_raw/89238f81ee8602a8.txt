Kingman County Kansas Sheriff's Office | Welcome to the Kingman County Sheriff’s Office The mission of the Kingman County Sheriffs Office is to assure a safe environment for the community and to enhance the quality of life of the citizens of Kingman County, Kansas. We will uphold the law fairly and firmly, pursue and bring to justice those who break the law, to keep the peace, prevent crime, and help reassure the citizens we serve with integrity, common sense and sound judgment. We value and respect the rights and dignity of all persons and exhibit professionalism and empathy in our contact with the public. We shall constantly strive to achieve these objectives and ideals, dedicating ourselves to the citizens of Kingman County, Kansas.
