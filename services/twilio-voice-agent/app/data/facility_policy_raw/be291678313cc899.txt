In the last 185 years since the first Sheriff took office, the position has been graced by 37 leaders, representing more than just law and order in Berrien County. Some sheriffs were farmers, others were politicians and law men, one was a woman, and even a Canadian and Scotsman, some moved away after serving while others lived and died in Berrien County.
