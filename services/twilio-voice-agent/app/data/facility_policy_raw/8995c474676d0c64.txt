Autauga County: Metro Jail Skip to main content Home / County Departments / Sheriff's Department / Metro Jail Metro Jail Autauga County Metro Jail Jail Administrator Captain Larry Nixon Photo by Deputy Sheriff Troy Seamon The Autauga County Metro Jail is a state of the art facility administered by Captain Larry Nixon. It opened in May of 2004 replacing the old jail that was built in 1970. Metro has 138 beds, 40 employees, and houses both city and county inmates.
