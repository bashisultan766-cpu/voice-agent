Website Sign In Search Home Departments Sheriff's Office Sheriff's Office The Sheriff's Office consists of deputies and civilian personnel in three major divisions: Administration, Court Services, and Corrections. We maintain the custody, care, control and movement of inmates; serve civil and criminal processes and court orders; maintain security for court facilities, judges, jurors and other trial participants. CALEA Accreditation Defined Polar Plunge Sheriff Bowden and the Hampton Sheriff’s Office participated in the 2026 Polar Plunge for Special Olympics. The funds raised will provide year-round sports training, competition, leadership opportunities, and health services to 23,000 athletes with disabilities. Virginia Sheriff’s Association’s Legislative Day Sheriff Bowden joined over 150 sheriffs, deputy sheriffs, and legislative partners at the State Capitol for Virginia Sheriff’s Association annual Legislative Day. Wythe Exchange Club Awards The Wythe Exchange Club hosted a dinner and awards ceremony to recognize their community partnership with Sheriff Karen E. Bowden and the Hampton Sheriff’s Office. Captain B. Moore was presented with the Sheriff’s Award. Sergeant W. Mouton was presented with the Deputy of the Year Award. Mrs. J. Robinson was presented with Outstanding Performance Award. Mr. W. Badgett was presented with the Civilian of the Year Award. Deputy K. Cameron was presented with the Community Service Award. The Transportation Unit was presented with the Esprit de Corps Award. Staff Promotions Sheriff Bowden and the Hampton Sheriff’s Office are proud to announce the latest staff promotions in a closed ceremony. Deputy B. Weiss and Deputy T. Warren were promoted to the rank of Master Deputy. Deputy S. Mays and Deputy M. Borden were promoted to the rank of Lieutenant. Reading Night Sheriff Bowden and the Hampton Sheriff’s Office attended Burbank Elementary’s Lucky to Love Reading Family Fun Night. The Hampton Sheriff’s Office brought the Easter Bunny to read with the kids and give out Junior Deputy stickers. Academy Graduation The Hampton Sheriff’s Office proudly announces our graduates from the Hampton Roads Criminal Justice Training Academy in Newport News. Congratulations to the Class 26-1! Veterans Care Project Sheriff Bowden and the Hampton Sheriff’s Office joined the Veterans Care Project to serve meals to veterans and staff at Hampton VA Hospital. Prescription Drug Take Back Sheriff Bowden and the Hampton Sheriff’s Office partnered with the DEA for National Prescription Drug Take Back. The event provides Hampton citizens with the opportunity to disposing unused medication. National Correctional Officers and Nurses Week The Hampton Sheriff’s Office planned a week full of festivities to show our appreciation for our deputies and nurses. Senior Celebration The Hampton Sheriff’s Office sponsored its 18th annual Senior Celebration to celebrate Older Americans month.
