Staff We are comprised of eighteen full-time deputies. These deputies include road patrol, a K9 unit, and a deputy assigned to the Multi-Area Narcotics (MAN) task force. The full-time deputies are assisted by groups of volunteers such as the Special Deputies, Mounted Deputies, Search & Rescue/UTV Deputies, and the Dive Team. All these individuals work together to protect the citizens and visitors of Williams County to the best of their ability.
