Johnson County Sheriff | Johnson County Kansas Skip to main content Johnson County Sheriff's Office Follow us on: Welcome Message from the Sheriff Thank you for visiting the Johnson County Sheriff’s Office website. It is an honor to serve and protect this community alongside a dedicated team of professionals. As your Sheriff, I believe that true public safety is achieved through a partnership between law enforcement and the community. Together, we can make Johnson County a safer, more connected place for everyone to live, work, and thrive. Community Collaboration Our success relies on strong relationships with the residents we serve. Your trust, involvement, and feedback are essential to identifying and addressing issues that affect our neighborhoods. Whether it’s participating in community events, Sheriff’s Office educational opportunities or sharing concerns, your engagement makes a difference. We encourage open communication and are committed to being transparent and accessible in all that we do. Embracing Technology In today’s rapidly evolving world, staying ahead in law enforcement means leveraging innovative tools and technology to solve complex crime problems. Our office is dedicated to adopting cutting-edge solutions that enhance public safety, streamline processes, and improve our crime fighting abilities. From advanced data analysis to modern surveillance techniques, these resources empower us to better protect our community and solve crimes more effectively. Professionalism and Innovation Our team operates with the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and accountability. We are constantly seeking new ways to improve our services through collaboration with neighboring agencies, businesses, and community partners. By sharing resources and expertise, we can address challenges more efficiently and foster a culture of continuous improvement. A Shared Vision for a Safer Community Together, we can build a future where innovation and cooperation drive meaningful change. I invite you to explore our website to learn more about the services we provide, the programs we offer, and the initiatives we’re implementing to enhance safety in Johnson County. Your involvement and support are invaluable to our mission. Thank you for trusting us to serve and protect this incredible community. We look forward to working with you to create a safer, stronger, and more connected Johnson County. Stay safe and stay engaged, Byron K. Roberson Sheriff, Johnson County Sheriff’s Office Our Mission To cultivate a law enforcement organization that thrives on exceptional leadership and open communication with a commitment to building partnerships with the citizens we serve. By fostering trust within our community and embracing innovative practices, we aim to create an environment built on collaboration, accountability, transparency, and professionalism. Through these efforts, we will ensure the safety and well-being of both staff, and the community we serve. Our Vision To be a premier law enforcement agency, demonstrating effective leadership, building community partnerships, promoting trust, transparency, and embracing innovation.
