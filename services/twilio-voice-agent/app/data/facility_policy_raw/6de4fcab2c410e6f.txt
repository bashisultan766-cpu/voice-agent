Flood Control Recorded Deeds/Liens Recreation Stormwater Management Stray or Loose Dogs Street Lighting Tour Carson City UNR Extension Visitors Bureau APPLIANCE PICK UP LEARN MORE Carson City Sheriff's Office Carson City Sheriff's Office Carson City Sheriff's Office Carson City Sheriff's Office Carson City Sheriff's Office Welcome to the Carson City Sheriff’s Office. The men and women of this agency are dedicated to the very highest levels of professional law enforcement services possible and committed to your safety. The agency's most basic philosophy is to be an active participant in the community. What this means to you is that we are driven to keep you informed about your home, neighborhood, work, and everything that is taking place around you in Carson City. In return, we ask that you provide us with information; together, we can make our community a safe and comfortable environment to raise our children and enjoy the highest standards of living. It is not enough to want to become a member of the Sheriff’s team; our employees are dedicated to understanding your needs, so you receive the services you need. To achieve this, our goal is to enhance staff education and training opportunities and present to you the most experienced, qualified, and capable employees possible. I hope that you will use and enjoy this site as a public safety information platform. Please remember that the quality of your law enforcement agency is directly tied to the feedback from those we serve. We appreciate your feedback .
