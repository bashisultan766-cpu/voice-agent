About Aurora Plains Academy Aurora Plains Academy is located in Plankinton, South Dakota. Treatment areas for the youth at the Academy include mental illness, emotional problems, conduct problems, cognitive delays, addictions, sexual abuse/offender issues, academic issues, and peer and family problems.
